Lil Tecca

Tyler-Justin Anthony Sharpe, known professionally as Lil Tecca, is an American rapper, singer and songwriter. He gained recognition upon releasing his breakout single, "Ransom", which peaked at number four on the Billboard Hot 100. He has also previously released songs that have garnered attention such as "Love Me", "Molly Girl", "Did It Again"
